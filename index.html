Install on Phone (PWA)
Add to home screen for native experience
📱 iOS (Safari)
Tap the Share button → scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. AttendX will appear as an app icon on your home screen.
🤖 Android (Chrome)
Tap the 3-dot menu → tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" → tap Install. Works as a full-screen app.
💻 Desktop (Chrome/Edge)
Look for the install icon in the address bar (right side) → click it → click Install.
